# Hacienda Del Sol Brand Voice Guide

## Brand Voice Summary

Hacienda Del Sol speaks with the voice of a warm, grounded wellness sanctuary. The tone is nurturing, immersive, and emotionally restorative, while still offering clear structure around retreats, programs, nourishment, movement, hydrotherapy, and personalized support.

The brand should feel like a calm guide helping guests return to themselves. It should evoke safety, nature, nourishment, transformation, and deep restoration without becoming overly clinical, salesy, or generic.

The voice should feel:

- Warm
- Restorative
- Nature-led
- Spiritually aware
- Emotionally intelligent
- Personalized
- Gentle but confident
- Premium without sounding corporate
- Transformational without overpromising

## Core Brand Essence

Hacienda Del Sol is a wellness sanctuary in the Costa Rican jungle designed to help guests rest, reset, and reconnect with their body, mind, and spirit.

The brand is built around the idea that healing happens through nourishment, nature, safety, movement, rest, water, breath, meaningful connection, and sensory presence.

At its best, the copy should make the reader feel:

- Held
- Safe
- Cared for
- Invited
- Restored
- Reconnected with nature
- More grounded in their body
- Open to transformation

## Primary Audience

The brand speaks to people who are seeking restoration, renewal, and a meaningful wellness experience.

The audience may include:

- Guests feeling burned out or depleted
- People seeking a physical and emotional reset
- Solo travelers looking for a safe retreat environment
- Couples or groups seeking a curated wellness stay
- Wellness-oriented travelers who value nature, nourishment, and intentional experiences
- Guests interested in movement, clean food, bodywork, hydrotherapy, and nervous system support

The audience is not only looking for a vacation. They are looking for a reset, a sense of safety, and a deeper experience of well-being.

## Positioning

Hacienda Del Sol should be positioned as a secluded Costa Rican wellness resort where guests can design a personalized retreat experience rooted in nature, nourishment, and nervous system restoration.

The brand should not feel like a standard hotel, spa, medical clinic, or fitness retreat.

It should feel like:

- A sanctuary
- A healing environment
- A curated wellness experience
- A place for restoration and reconnection
- A retreat that supports the whole person

## Messaging Pillars

### 1. Come Home to Yourself

The brand frequently returns to the idea of homecoming. This is not just about arriving at a beautiful property. It is about returning to the body, the self, and the inner light.

Use this theme when writing about:

- Emotional reset
- Rest
- Nervous system safety
- Reconnection
- Personal transformation
- The deeper meaning of the retreat experience

Sample language:

- Return to yourself
- Come home to your body
- Feel held by nature
- Reconnect with your inner light
- Rest into a deeper sense of safety
- Find your way back to balance

### 2. Nature as a Healing Force

Nature is not just the setting. It is part of the experience.

The jungle, ocean, fresh air, sunlight, water, wildlife, and natural rhythms should be treated as active elements of the retreat. The land is described as wise, restorative, and supportive.

Use this theme when writing about:

- The Costa Rican setting
- The jungle
- Ocean proximity
- Hydrotherapy
- Outdoor movement
- Sunlight
- Sensory experiences
- Slow living and natural rhythms

Sample language:

- Surrounded by the healing rhythm of the jungle
- Held by the land
- Restored by nature
- Guided by the pace of the natural world
- Immersed in the sights, sounds, and textures of Costa Rica
- Supported by fresh air, sunlight, water, and stillness

### 3. Nervous System Restoration

The brand’s wellness philosophy centers strongly on the nervous system. This should be one of the clearest strategic anchors in future content.

Use this theme when writing about:

- Burnout
- Stress recovery
- Rest
- Breathwork
- Hydrotherapy
- Movement
- Nourishment
- Safety
- Sleep
- Emotional regulation

Sample language:

- Support your nervous system
- Create space for deep restoration
- Help the body shift out of survival mode
- Restore a sense of safety
- Nourish the body from the inside out
- Slow down enough to truly reset

Avoid making medical claims. The language should suggest support, restoration, and well-being rather than guaranteed treatment or cure.

### 4. Personalized Wellness

The brand emphasizes flexibility and customization. Guests can design their retreat, choose add-ons, select wellness packs, arrive any day, and enjoy a flexible schedule.

Use this theme when writing about:

- Retreat design
- Guest experience
- Add-ons
- Discovery calls
- Food
- Wellness packs
- Daily flow
- Individual needs

Sample language:

- Design a retreat that meets you where you are
- Choose the level of support your body is asking for
- Build on your core retreat experience
- Follow a rhythm that feels right for you
- Receive support tailored to your needs
- Create a stay that feels deeply personal

### 5. Nourishment on Every Level

Food is positioned as clean, delicious, personalized, and part of the healing experience. Nourishment also extends beyond food into movement, rest, nature, touch, and connection.

Use this theme when writing about:

- Meals
- Juices
- Clean eating
- Culinary experience
- Spa treatments
- Movement
- Emotional care
- Guest support

Sample language:

- Nourish your body, mind, and spirit
- Clean, delicious meals prepared with care
- Food that feels both satisfying and supportive
- Details designed to restore and replenish
- A full-body experience of nourishment
- Support that feeds every layer of your well-being

### 6. Gentle Transformation

The brand is transformational, but not harsh or aggressive. Transformation should feel gentle, warm, and possible.

Use this theme when writing about:

- Retreat outcomes
- Program benefits
- Guest journeys
- Burnout recovery
- Mind-body-spirit reset
- Long-term change

Sample language:

- Leave feeling lighter and more grounded
- Create space for meaningful change
- Reset your rhythm
- Reconnect with what matters
- Step into a renewed sense of vitality
- Let the body lead the way back to balance

Avoid language that feels too forceful, like:

- Crush your goals
- Hack your body
- Transform overnight
- Fix yourself
- Cure burnout
- Lose weight forever
- Guaranteed results

## Tone Attributes

### Warm

The voice should feel human, kind, and welcoming.

Use:

- We invite you
- You are welcome to
- A place to rest
- A sanctuary for renewal
- Thoughtfully prepared
- Gently supported

Avoid:

- Transactional language
- Overly formal hospitality language
- Cold or corporate phrasing

### Grounded

Even when the copy is spiritual or emotional, it should remain connected to real experiences: meals, movement, water, rest, breath, bodywork, nature, and daily rhythms.

Use:

- Daily movement classes
- Clean meals
- Breathwork
- Saltwater pool
- Private bungalows
- Guided support
- Hydrotherapy circuit

Avoid copy that becomes too abstract or mystical without grounding it in the guest experience.

### Sensory

The voice should help the reader feel the place.

Use references to:

- Sunlight
- Fresh air
- Warm water
- Cool plunges
- Jungle sounds
- Ocean air
- Clean food
- Soft textures
- Stillness
- Breath
- Movement

Sample language:

- Wake to the rhythm of the jungle
- Move slowly into the day
- Rest under the warmth of the sun
- Let the water soften your body
- Taste food that feels clean, fresh, and alive

### Emotionally Restorative

The copy should acknowledge stress, depletion, disconnection, and burnout without sounding heavy or fear-based.

Use:

- If you have been feeling depleted
- When your body is asking for rest
- For moments when you need to feel held
- A place to soften, reset, and begin again
- Support for the parts of you that have been running on empty

Avoid:

- Fear-based urgency
- Shame
- Pressure
- Aggressive transformation claims

### Premium but Intimate

The brand should feel high-quality, but not flashy. The luxury is in care, nature, privacy, nourishment, and intentional details.

Use:

- Private bungalows
- Chef-designed meals
- Curated retreats
- Personalized support
- Thoughtful details
- Intimate sanctuary
- Restorative experience

Avoid:

- Ultra-luxury clichés
- Status-driven language
- Excessive use of words like exclusive, elite, lavish, or opulent

## Sentence Style

The brand can use lyrical, emotional writing, but it should remain clear and easy to understand.

### Recommended Structure

Use a mix of:

- Short emotional lines for emphasis
- Medium-length explanatory sentences
- Sensory descriptions
- Clear benefit-driven paragraphs
- Simple CTAs

### Avoid

- Long, overpacked spiritual paragraphs
- Too many abstract ideas in one sentence
- Excessive adjectives
- Repetition of the same emotional promise
- Clinical language that makes the brand sound like a medical facility

Good CTAs should feel:

## Brand Voice Do and Don’t

### Do

- Speak directly to the guest.
- Make the reader feel safe, welcomed, and supported.
- Connect wellness benefits to real retreat elements.
- Use nature as part of the emotional and sensory experience.
- Emphasize personalization and flexibility.
- Use warm, grounded, human language.
- Keep transformation gentle and realistic.
- Include compliance language when discussing health-related programs.
- Balance emotional copy with practical details.

### Don’t

- Sound like a hospital, clinic, or medical program.
- Promise cures or guaranteed health outcomes.
- Use fear-based wellness marketing.
- Overuse spiritual language without grounding it.
- Make the copy feel too salesy.
- Use generic wellness clichés.
- Push urgency too hard.
- Overstate the science behind services.
- Make weight loss or body transformation sound shame-based.

## Compliance and Health Claim Guardrails

Hacienda Del Sol should always be careful with health-related language.

The website clearly states that Hacienda Del Sol is not a medical facility, does not have medically trained staff, does not provide medical advice, and asks participants to get clearance from their doctor before joining programs.

Future copy should follow the same standard.

### Safe Language

Use phrases like:

- May support
- Designed to support
- Can help create space for
- Intended to encourage
- Offers a restorative environment for
- Supports the body’s natural processes
- Helps guests feel more grounded
- Encourages rest and renewal

### Avoid

- Cures
- Treats
- Diagnoses
- Reverses
- Guarantees
- Medically proven
- Heals disease
- Eliminates inflammation
- Fixes hormones
- Solves trauma
- Permanent weight loss
- Burn fat forever

### Recommended Disclaimer Style

Hacienda Del Sol is not a medical facility and does not provide medical advice. Guests are responsible for consulting their doctor before participating in any retreat, treatment, cleanse, movement class, or wellness program, especially if they have health conditions that require medical supervision.

## Example Brand Voice Applications

### Website Intro Example

Step into a slower rhythm, surrounded by the healing beauty of the Costa Rican jungle. At Hacienda Del Sol, each retreat is designed to help you rest deeply, nourish your body, and reconnect with the inner clarity that modern life often makes hard to hear.

### Retreat Description Example

This retreat is for guests who feel depleted, overstimulated, or ready for a meaningful reset. Through clean meals, daily movement, hydrotherapy, bodywork, nature, and personalized support, you are invited to soften your pace and return to a more grounded sense of vitality.

### Email Intro Example

If your body has been asking for rest, this is your invitation to listen. Hacienda Del Sol offers a peaceful retreat experience rooted in nature, nourishment, and nervous system restoration, giving you space to step away from daily pressure and reconnect with yourself.

### Social Caption Example

A few days in the jungle can change the way your body remembers rest. Clean meals, warm water, fresh air, slow mornings, and the quiet support of nature come together to create space for renewal.

### Ad Copy Example

Design a wellness retreat that meets you where you are. Rest, nourish, move, and reset in a secluded Costa Rican sanctuary created for deep restoration.

## Writing Checklist

Before publishing any Hacienda Del Sol content, check that the copy:

- Feels warm, calm, and restorative
- Connects the retreat experience to nature
- Mentions real guest benefits without overpromising
- Keeps wellness language grounded and compliant
- Avoids aggressive sales language
- Reflects personalization and care
- Uses sensory language when appropriate
- Makes the guest feel safe and invited
- Includes practical details when needed
- Sounds human, not generic or automated

## One-Sentence Brand Voice Anchor

Hacienda Del Sol speaks like a warm, grounded guide inviting guests into a personalized wellness sanctuary where nature, nourishment, rest, and gentle support help them come home to themselves.
